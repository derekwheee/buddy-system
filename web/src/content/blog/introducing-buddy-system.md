**Buddy System** is lab equipment that wants to be touched: a clean black panel, a fresh
white breadboard, a handful of colored jumpers, and the feeling that nothing you do is
permanent.

## Why another modular system?

Because the breadboard is already the best prototyping surface in the world — we just gave
it a control unit and a family of color-coded cards that snap right onto the pitch.

## What's in the box

- A **Buddy Base** — the central control unit
- A starter set of **Buddy Cards** — Pots, Clock, and Filter to begin with
- Jumpers, in the family colors

Every module is somebody's Buddy. More soon.
